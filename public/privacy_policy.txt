CART (LOCAL STORAGE)

Your shopping cart is stored locally in your browser using localStorage.
This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. It is
cleared if you clear your browser data.

---

BROWSING THE CATALOG

Product listings are fetched from Fourthwall on your behalf through our
server. Browsing the catalog does not send any personal data to us or
to Fourthwall beyond a standard request for product information.

---

CHECKOUT

When you click "checkout," our server asks Fourthwall to create a cart
containing only the item IDs and quantities you selected, and you are
then redirected to Fourthwall (https://fourthwall.king-tajin.dev) to
complete your purchase. Any personal or payment information you enter
at checkout - name, address, email, card details, and so on - is
submitted directly to Fourthwall and governed by their own privacy
policy. We receive only the order information necessary for
fulfillment. We do not control or take responsibility for Fourthwall's
data practices.

---

ANALYTICS

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which collects anonymous,
aggregated traffic data. It does not use cookies, does not track you
across sites, and does not collect any personal information. For more
information see https://www.cloudflare.com/web-analytics/

---

COOKIES

store.king-tajin.dev does not use cookies.

---

YOUR RIGHTS

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the
right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we
hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email
addresses above.

If you are located in California, you have rights under the California
Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell your personal data.
